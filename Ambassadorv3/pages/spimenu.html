51K IPA – 12 oz
Blackrocks Brewery Marquette, MI ABV 7% IBU 68
Blackrocks Brewery donates a portion of sales of this earthy grapefruit, apricot, and piney hop flavored American IPA to support the building and maintenance of Upper Michigan recreational trails.

Oatmeal Cream Pie Ale – 12 oz
Pigeon Hill Brewing Co. Muskegon, MI ABV 6% IBU 10
Using oats, marshmallow, and other secret ingredients, O.C.P. was born.
Tasting notes: Seriously, it tastes just like oatmeal creme pie cookies.

Edmund Fitzgerald Porter – 12 oz
Great Lakes Brewing Co. Cleveland, OH ABV 6% IBU 37
Robust and complex, this Porter is a bittersweet tribute to the legendary freighter’s fallen crew, with rich roasted barley and bittersweet chocolate-coffee notes.

Rogue River Brown – 12 oz
Rockford Brewing Company Rockford, MI ABV 6.5%
Winner of two Great American Beer Festival medals, this English-Style Brown Ale was made to be easy drinking with a warm dark chocolate and caramel complexity.

Paulaner München Hefe-Weizen – 11.7 oz
Paulaner Brauerei Munich, Germany ABV 5.5%
The Paulaner brew masters have perfected a unique technique with “yeast suspension”, resulting in a uniform slightly cloudy appearance, consistent quality and perfect taste.

Upper Hand Light American Lager – 12 oz
Upper Hand Brewery Escanaba, MI ABV 4.2%
Upper Hand Light is brewed with 100% barley malt, giving it a fuller, more authentic malt flavor than your standard American Light Lager. Upper Hand Light is clean, and light on the palate.
